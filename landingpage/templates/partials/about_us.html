About us

The concept

ImpactCount has a very simple concept with potentially large implications.
Employees will own the positive reviews and endorsements they receive directly from clients and colleagues. Employers can showcase the combined positive impact their employees have on clients.
We believe that giving employees control over their positive reviews and team endorsements creates a win-win situation for both parties, fostering stronger relationships and a more meaningful, motivated work environment.

The value

Great reviews are immensely valuable for companies.
So far, the same value has been largely inaccessible for employees. Millions of workers have a huge positive impact on their clients every day, while their true client-facing value is not theirs to truly leverage. This leads to an over-reliance on career paths and job titles.
Consider joining to unlock the true value of your achievements or your team's potential.
